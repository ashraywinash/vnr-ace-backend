Department of Chemistry
Our department seamlessly blends the foundational principles of chemistry with state-of-the-art technology to address modern challenges. From developing green technologies to innovating smart materials, we cultivate a spirit of creativity, collaboration, and sustainability, preparing graduates to break new ground. We are committed to transforming visionary ideas into practical solutions for a healthier, more sustainable future. Our experienced faculty, with academic and research expertise from prestigious institutions like NIT, IIT, HCU, IISc, and various state universities, is central to this mission. As the field of chemical sciences evolves rapidly, our department aims to strengthen its core by integrating with engineering disciplines, fostering a robust, interdisciplinary knowledge framework. Faculty conclaves, workshops, guest lectures, FDPs, conferences, symposiums, and seminars enable us to promote synergy between cutting-edge research methodologies.

Our department offers a range of theory and laboratory courses, including Chemistry for Engineering (CFE), Engineering Chemistry (EC), Environmental Science (ES), Happiness and Well-being (HW), and Engineering Chemistry lab for B. Tech students. Additionally, we offer open elective courses such as Introduction to Climate Change and Sustainable Chemistry for Engineers. Students are actively engaged in research through departmental Research Interest Groups (RIGs) and course-based projects.

Vision
Driving chemical innovation to tackle critical societal challenges while fostering a community of ethical and responsible chemists dedicated to sustainable development and enhancing human well-being.

Mission
Empowering professionals in chemistry to lead a sustainable future by driving research, innovation, and collaboration that address global challenges in energy, materials, and environmental sustainability

Goals & Objectives
Establish a strong foundation in chemistry by integrating innovative and interdisciplinary education, that bridges principles of chemistry with cutting-edge technology and engineering disciplines
Address societal challenges through promotion of sustainable practices fostering the development of green solutions and smart materials for a healthier & sustainable future
Drive advancements in chemical sciences and technology by encouraging high-quality research and innovation through collaboration and resource-sharing.
Prepare students for global competitiveness by equipping them with the knowledge and essential skills in solving challenges in modern chemical sciences and technology.
Shape graduates who are not only technically proficient but also creative, collaborative, and socially responsible, leading to contribute meaningfully towards society.
Cultivate a collaborative academic & research culture by leveraging the expertise of distinguished faculty with strong educational and research backgrounds from premier institutions such as-NIT, IIT, HCU, IISc, and state universities.
