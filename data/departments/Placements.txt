# VNRVJIET Placements and Training
The Institute has a dedicated Training and Placement (T&P) Cell that handles all placement-related activities.

## Placement Highlights
- VNRVJIET has a consistent placement record with over 90% of eligible students placed every year.
- Top recruiters include Google, Microsoft, Amazon, JPMC, Deloitte, TCS, Cognizant, and many more.
- The highest pay package offered is 97 LPA (International) and 49 LPA (Domestic by Google).
- The average pay package is approximately 11-12 LPA for CSE and related branches.
- Over 100+ companies visit the campus for recruitment every year.

## Internships
- Students are encouraged to take up internships from their 3rd year onwards.
- Highest internship stipend offered is Rs. 1.1 Lakh per month by Google.
- Many students receive Pre-Placement Offers (PPOs) after successful internships.

## Training Programs
- The T&P cell conducts regular training on Technical skills, Soft skills, Aptitude, and Coding.
- Special training for Hackathons and Coding Contests is provided by the TURING club.
- Mock interviews and career guidance sessions are conducted by Alumni and Industry experts.
